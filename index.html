Overview

This privacy policy applies to the iOS workout app (RepHeft) and explains how your information is handled. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data, usage data, or analytics of any kind. The App functions entirely on your device and does not transmit any information to us or any third party.

The App contains no advertisements and no advertising SDKs. We will never show ads or share your data with advertisers.

In-App Purchase

The App offers a free trial period so you can explore all features before committing. After the trial, a one-time in-app purchase unlocks lifetime access to the App.

All purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment details. For questions about billing, refunds, or purchase issues, please refer to Apple's support page.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, analytics tools, or SDKs that could collect data about you.

Children's Privacy

Since we collect no data whatsoever, the App is safe for users of all ages.
